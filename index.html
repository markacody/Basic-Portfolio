About Me

Two questions get Mark up in the morning. "Why does this have to be so hard?"" And "Can't this be more fun?"

Mark's career is about finding answers. They are, after all, life's most basic questions.

As an analyst, Mark can make sense of any mess. When it comes to the web and to information systems, many people may be tricked into thinking the mess is limited to a user interface, but a closer look almost always finds the broader context. User experience includes the processes and encounters that lead into and follow after close contact with an application.

Solution Designer. Stories, experience, and expectations matter. Impact is everything. A solution that takes root and makes change is more than an application. It's a better version of real life. It's more compelling than reality.

Developer. Not just a dreamer, Mark makes designs come to life as User Experience Designer with Full Stack Development skills.
